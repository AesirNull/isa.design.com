Summary

Mentis was a videogame project with a focus on adventuring through an alien landscape while pushing back against a new unknown threat.
One of my roles in this project was to develop a weapon that reflected the story through its design, hence why you see two versions of it.
The red and black version belongs to the enemy faction, which is why it's made of sharp, inorganic shapes. The purple and green version belongs to the protagonist, and the sillouette changes to reflect that with the addition of the vines.

Summary

NEXUS was a wayfinding project, revolving around the revitalizating of a sports club center through the creation of social spaces that would invite community members to bond and express themselves through digital street art. For that purpose, I built a mobile app that allows users to save and share artwork they create, look at the community and participate in organized events with others. It would also serve to book tickets for games happening in the stadium and watch the event highlight as they are streamed online, as well as read any articles available in the club's archives.
